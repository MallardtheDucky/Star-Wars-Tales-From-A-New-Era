The State of the Galaxy

Everything before the Battle of Endor remains as you know it. But the deaths of the Rebel Strike Team on Endor changed the trajectory of the galaxy forever.

From the ashes of the old Empire rose Emperor Draxus Thorne, a charismatic visionary who united the Remnants through cunning, meritocracy, and ruthless efficiency. He formed the Galactic Imperium, stripping away the xenophobia of the past to build a terrifyingly competent war machine.

In response, the New Republic struggles. Stretched thin, marred by corruption, and reeling from the shocking execution of Grandmaster Luke Skywalker, they frantically build their fleets to meet the northern threat.

Now, in 17 ABY, an uneasy Cold War has settled. The New Jedi Order hides on Devaron, while the dark Order of the Imperial Templars rules Yavin 4. War is inevitable. Who will you fight for?

Historical Archives

The definitive record of events following the Battle of Endor up to the current Cold War in 17 ABY. Legends continuity applies to pre-Clone Wars lore.

4 ABY

The Ultimate Sacrifice at Endor

A strike team led by Leia Organa, Han Solo, and Chewbacca lands on Endor. Overwhelmed by stormtroopers despite Ewok assistance, and corralled into the bunker, the heroes make the ultimate sacrifice, detonating the shield generator with themselves inside. The Rebel fleet destroys the Death Star II.

Luke redeems Vader, Palpatine dies. Mourning his friends, Luke is guided by Force ghosts to rebuild a New Jedi Order—one devoid of the old dogma, allowing attachments.

Operation: Cinder Activated

The Galactic Concordance

Gallius Rex gathers Imperial forces at Jakku for a suicidal stand. Foiled and shot by Rae Sloane, Rex dies. Sloane, seeing the Empire's end, takes her own life. Mas Amedda signs the Galactic Concordance, officially ending the Galactic Civil War.

The New Republic forms. While some Imperials integrate via the Amnesty Program, others form warlord fiefdoms or the "Shadow Council" in the Northern Outer Rim.

5 ABY
9 ABY

Rise of the Galactic Imperium

Following Moff Gideon's death, Moff Draxus Thorne poisons the Shadow Council on Bastion, taking absolute control. He purges incompetence, implementing meritocracy.

Draxus invades Mandalore, executing Din Djarin, Grogu, and most Mandalorians. He declares the Galactic Imperium, initiating a 3-year rapid expansion in the north utilizing World Devastators and automation.

Controversially, Draxus allows aliens into the Imperial Army and Navy, and near-humans into the Stormtrooper Corps, bolstering his forces immensely.

The Fall of the Grandmaster

The New Jedi Order flourishes on Yavin 4, located deep in Imperium territory. Emperor Thorne forms the Order of the Imperial Templars with a darksider mercenary.

Thorne fakes diplomacy on Yavin 4, then launches a massive invasion using jamming tech. Overwhelmed, Luke Skywalker surrenders himself to secure his students' escape.

Luke is taken to Bastion, encased in carbonite, and publicly decapitated by Emperor Thorne.

The Jedi flee to Devaron. The New Republic, terrified by Thorne's power, begins rapid militarization.

12 ABY
17 ABY

The Cold War

Present day. The Galactic Imperium fully controls the North/Northeast. The Imperial Templars rule from the former Jedi Temple on Yavin 4.

The New Republic and Imperium are locked in an intense Cold War, building armies, engaging in espionage, and waiting for the spark that will ignite the galaxy into full-scale war.

Galactic Powers

Choose your allegiance. The fate of the galaxy rests on the impending conflict.

Galactic Imperium

Capital: Bastion Emperor: Draxus Thorne

A reborn, highly efficient autocracy built on the ashes of Palpatine's Empire. Emperor Thorne has eradicated the nepotism and xenophobia of the past, creating a meritocracy. Aliens serve in the Army and Navy, and automation feeds an endless war machine.

Military Structure

  • Imperial Navy: Integrated, massive, heavily automated.
  • Stormtrooper Corps: Elite offensive force (Humans & Near-Humans).
  • Imperial Army: Standard defense force (All species welcome).

New Republic

Capital: Chandrila Gov: Galactic Senate

The legitimate democratic government of the galaxy. Though vast, it is plagued by bureaucratic slowness, internal corruption from integrated Imperial officials, and military thinness. Jolted awake by the death of Luke Skywalker, they are rapidly militarizing.

Current State

  • Military: Rapidly expanding shipyards, high focus on starfighters.
  • Politics: Divided senate, struggling to manage outer rim security.
  • Perception: Viewed by some as weak, but possesses vast industrial might.

Imperial Templars

Base: Yavin 4 Allegiance: Emperor Thorne

A dark-side Force order acting as the Imperium's elite enforcers and peacekeepers. Formed by a mercenary trained by a fallen Order 66 survivor. They occupy the former Jedi Praxeum on Yavin 4, recruiting inquisitors, dark Jedi, and dark-side cultists to serve the Emperor's will.

New Jedi Order

Base: Devaron Grandmaster: Vacant

Severely wounded by the public execution of Grandmaster Luke Skywalker, the survivors retreated to Devaron. Built on Luke's reforms—allowing attachments, rejecting dogma, and welcoming diverse light-side sects—the Order now operates as an independent vigilante force seeking to honor Luke's sacrifice.

Roleplay Rules

PTK and RTK Systems

Permission to Kill (PTK): Before taking a character’s life, you must obtain explicit permission from the player controlling them.

Reason to Kill (RTK): If a situation realistically leads to death and provides no plausible escape, RTK will be used. The reason must be clear and justified within RP context.

Unclear situations? Ping an available staff member for guidance.

OP Roleplaying is Prohibited

  • Auto-Hitting: Hitting/injuring someone without their consent to dictate the outcome.
  • Auto-Dodging: Dodging every attack without giving others a chance to influence the outcome.
  • Metagaming: Using OOC knowledge in IC situations.
  • Forcing Actions/Coercion: Compelling characters to act against their personality, goals, or player consent.

Roleplay Content Guidelines

Minimum Effort: Three Sentence Minimum per post. One-liners are discouraged and consistent use results in staff intervention.

AI Content: Strictly Prohibited. All RP posts, sheets, and narrative elements must be handwritten. Violations lead to warnings or removal.

Multiple RPs: A single character may participate in up to 3 roleplays concurrently, provided timelines align logically without conflict.

Medical Conditions: Must be handled with accuracy and sensitivity {=Daddy Please=}. Research thoroughly to avoid misrepresentation. Feedback on poor portrayals must be accepted, or characters risk removal.

Combat (CRP) & Erotic (ERP) Roleplay

CRP: Highly encouraged. Plan scenes fairly, avoid godmodding, and respect combat outcomes.

ERP: Permitted, but MUST be kept in threads and tagged appropriately. Consent is required IC and OOC.

NSFW Restrictions

As an 18+ server, some themes are heavily restricted or outright banned.

  • Restricted (extreme-hentai channel ONLY): Zoophilia, Heavy Abuse, Amputee.
  • BANNED (Immediate Warn/Ban): Necrophilia, Loli/Shota, IRL Zoophilia, Heavy Gore/Guro, Scat, Vore.
